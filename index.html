Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Your privacy is fundamental to ipsum. We believe your reflections and emotional data belong exclusively to you. This policy outlines exactly how your data is handled.

  • Local Storage (SwiftData): Your mood entries, feelings, moments, and photos are stored locally on your device. We do not currently sync this personal data to our servers.
  • AI Generation (Google Gemini): To generate your personal prose portrait (the "Ipsum"), the app temporarily sends your recent feelings and mood summaries to the Google Gemini API. This data is transmitted securely and is strictly used to generate the text. We do not use your personal entries to train public AI models.
  • Authentication & Analytics (Firebase): We use Firebase Authentication (Email/Password and Google Sign-In) to manage your account. We also use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (like screens viewed or features used) to help us improve the app. This data does not include the content of your personal journals.
  • Camera & Photo Library: The app requests access to your camera and photo library strictly for you to attach images to your Moments. We do not access photos you do not explicitly select.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the Settings menu within the app.

If you have any questions about your privacy, please contact us using the support email above.
